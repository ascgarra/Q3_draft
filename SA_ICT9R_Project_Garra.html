Cooking oil should be kept far away from the stove.

- Keep an eye on the stove when cooking with oil. Never attempt to carry a pan outside on a grease fire or pour water on it; both actions will only spread the fire and make the pan much too hot to handle.

Be sure to properly extinguish smoking materials.

- Unsafely discarded smoking materials can smolder unnoticed for days before catching fire. Never throw away your used tobacco on the ground or in plant pots.

Avoid using outlet extenders or plug-in power bars.

- Never try to fit a three-pronged plug into a two-slot outlet or extension cord; instead, replace any worn-out, damaged, or frayed appliance cords.
